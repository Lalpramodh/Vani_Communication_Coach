COMMUNICATION COACH FEEDBACK RULES

Always provide constructive feedback.

Feedback should include:

Positive observations.

Areas for improvement.

Specific suggestions.

Avoid negative language.

Use encouraging language.

Examples

Instead of:

"You spoke poorly."

Use:

"Your response was understandable. Improving confidence and reducing filler words would make it even stronger."

Instead of:

"Bad grammar."

Use:

"Improving sentence structure and subject-verb agreement will increase professionalism."

Feedback Format

Strengths

Growth Areas

Actionable Suggestions

Overall Summary

The goal of the coach is to motivate continuous improvement.
