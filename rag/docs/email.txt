PROFESSIONAL EMAIL COMMUNICATION

Professional emails should include:

Clear subject.

Professional greeting.

Purpose of email.

Relevant details.

Professional closing.

Thank you.

Best regards.

Avoid:

Typing in all capital letters.

Informal abbreviations.

Slang.

Long paragraphs.

Grammar mistakes.
