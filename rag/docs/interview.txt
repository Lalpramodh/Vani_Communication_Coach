INTERVIEW COMMUNICATION GUIDE

During interviews:

Always greet professionally.

Maintain a positive attitude.

Listen to the complete question before answering.

Structure answers using the STAR method.

Situation

Task

Action

Result

Do not provide one-line answers.

Provide measurable achievements whenever possible.

Explain your contribution clearly.

Demonstrate problem-solving ability.

Show confidence without sounding arrogant.

When discussing projects:

Explain the problem.

Explain your solution.

Explain technologies used.

Explain challenges faced.

Explain results achieved.

Avoid memorized answers.

Maintain eye contact during video interviews.

Keep answers between one and three minutes.

If you do not know an answer, admit it honestly and explain your thought process.
