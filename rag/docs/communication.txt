COMMUNICATION FITNESS GUIDELINES

Effective communication is the ability to express ideas clearly, confidently, and professionally.

Core Principles

1. Speak with confidence.
Avoid hesitation and excessive filler words such as "um", "uh", "like", and "you know".

2. Keep responses structured.
Every answer should have a beginning, middle, and conclusion.

3. Be concise.
Avoid unnecessary details while ensuring all important points are covered.

4. Use professional language.
Avoid slang, informal expressions, and overly casual language in professional conversations.

5. Listen carefully before responding.
Understand the question completely before answering.

6. Support your statements with examples whenever possible.

7. Maintain logical flow.
Each sentence should naturally connect with the previous one.

8. Demonstrate positive body language when speaking.
Maintain eye contact, smile naturally, and avoid distracting movements.

9. Speak at a moderate pace.
Speaking too quickly reduces clarity while speaking too slowly reduces engagement.

10. End responses confidently.
